| Type                                        | Audible Pattern                              | Example                         | Key Acoustic Features                                                                          | How It Differs from Others              |
| ------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| **Sound Repetition (Part-word repetition)** | Rapid repetition of first sound              | b-b-b-ball                      | Short repeated bursts (100–300 ms), similar waveform pattern, small gaps between repetitions   | Repeats only phoneme, not full syllable |
| **Syllable Repetition**                     | Repetition of full syllable                  | ba-ba-baby                      | Repeated syllable energy blocks, clear vowel repetition, longer than phoneme repetition        | Entire syllable repeated                |
| **Prolongation**                            | Stretched sound held for long duration       | ssssssnake                      | Continuous sustained frequency band, extended phoneme duration (1–5 sec), stable spectral band | No repetition, just extension           |
| **Block (Silent or Audible)**               | Sudden stop before word, may include tension | ———ball                         | Silence >500 ms before word OR strained low-energy airflow, sudden burst onset                 | No sound during silent block            |
| **Interjection (Filler)**                   | Inserted extra sound or word                 | um… I think                     | Mid-frequency stable tone, nasal vowel (“um”), short pause before/after                        | Added word, not repetition              |
| **Phrase Repetition**                       | Repetition of multiple words                 | I went to — I went to the store | Repeated long waveform segment, similar prosody and pitch contour                              | Multi-word repetition                   |
| **Cluttering**                              | Very fast, unclear, compressed speech        | iwenttothestore                 | High syllable rate (>6–7 syll/sec), reduced vowel duration, irregular rhythm                   | Rate-based dysfluency, not repetition   |
| **Normal Speech**                           | Smooth continuous flow                       | I went to the store             | Stable rhythm, natural pauses, no abnormal repetitions or prolongations                        | Baseline fluent speech                  |
